Granite

Granite.js is a front-end component library from addapptation, built to render JSON objects as DOM elements.

Download Github

Currently: V1.4.6

Micros

Granite micros give you the freedom to bring your own vision to life. The predefined JSON values give your team the ability to streamline your design and development process. Each block of granite.js creates a self-contained chunk of HTML, CSS, and JS that can be late-loaded into any front-end environment - yes, even inside your existing Vue, React, or Angular project.

Installation

Quickly add Granite to your project using our CDN. Copy and past the CSS link into the head of your document and include the JS script before the closing of your body tag.

Remember to update the micro you're referencing at the end of the url.

granite_[micro].js 
<script src="https://deployment-engine.herokuapp.com/storage?action_type=get_blob&container_name=addapptation&blob_name=granite/granite_[micro].js"></script>
